Champions

A couple of decades ago, some of us took time off from role-playing games to do the parenting thing, and raise a new generation of gamers. In September of 2015 I decided that the gaming hiatus had lasted long enough. Champions edition 6 was recently released, so I invited friends and family to submit 150pt "competent normal" characters, along with some ideas about what might happen to their character should a radioactive hippopotomus bite them.
